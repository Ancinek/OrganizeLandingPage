Organize app


Be amazed by the time you lost not using Organize app. Beautifuly designed to always be there for you. Anytime you need it.

Old idea with fresh execution

Organizing your desktop is not a recent discovery. It has been used across many different platforms including Windows, Linux and OSX.
Organize team took the idea and reinvented it so it can be even more useful. We know once you try it, there is no way back!

Invisibly beautiful

We spent some time thinking about the perfect design of our app. A way, that will make you feel comfortable using it with any app that has been created. Implementing its activity as a background was the best idea we could have possible thought of. We want you to get it, quickly learn it and act like it has always been there. Waiting for your action.

Once launched, it will enrich and facilitate your everyday computer work.

Window drag

So what is Organize all about?
Drag and drop actions. That is all it is.
Grab your window and move it to the edge of the screen. Drop it and see how magic happens. Resizing has never been so easy.

File and folder drop

Here at Organize functionality is the key to everything. Simultaneously, we want the experience to be as intuitive as possible, without you needing to learn challenging options.
So if you can drag an open window, why don't do the same with closed files and folders? Yes, you heard it. Drag desired object to previously mentioned edges and see them open in a blink of an eye.

Your own sets

As developers we work on our computer stations all the time.
One of the main reasons Organize was created was to make your and ours everyday routine much easier. That is why we implemented Application Sets feature.

Add couple of apps you open in one desktop every once in a while, position them and assign a shortcut. From now on, anytime you open your mac you will be able to launch all of your apps in just one click. Isn't it awesome?
